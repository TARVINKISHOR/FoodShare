Creative Recipes to Reduce Food Waste

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • Leftover vegetables
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Cooking oil

Steps:

  1. Chop all vegetables.
  2. Heat oil and saut√© garlic.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry.
  4. Add soy sauce and serve.
Bread Pudding

Ingredients:

  • Stale bread
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. Tear bread into pieces.
  2. Mix with milk, eggs, and sugar.
  3. Pour into baking dish.
  4. Bake until golden.
Smoothies

Ingredients:

  • Overripe fruits
  • Yogurt
  • Milk or juice

Steps:

  1. Add all ingredients to blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Serve chilled.
