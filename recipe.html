Creative Recipes to Reduce Food Waste

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • Leftover vegetables
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Cooking oil

Steps:

  1. Chop all vegetables.
  2. Heat oil and sauté garlic.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry.
  4. Add soy sauce and serve.
Bread Pudding

Ingredients:

  • Stale bread
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. Tear bread into pieces.
  2. Mix with milk, eggs, and sugar.
  3. Pour into baking dish.
  4. Bake until golden.
Smoothies

Ingredients:

  • Overripe fruits
  • Yogurt
  • Milk or juice

Steps:

  1. Add all ingredients to blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Serve chilled.
French Toast

Ingredients:

  • Stale bread slices
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Optional: sugar, cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Whisk eggs and milk (add sugar/cinnamon if desired).
  2. Dip bread slices into mixture.
  3. Fry on pan until golden brown.
Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • Leftover rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • 2 eggs
  • Soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Sauté vegetables in oil.
  2. Add leftover rice and mix.
  3. Scramble eggs and combine with rice. Season with soy sauce.
Mashed Potato Cakes

Ingredients:

  • Leftover mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Butter for frying

Steps:

  1. Mix mashed potatoes, cheese, and egg into a dough.
  2. Shape into patties.
  3. Fry in butter until golden.
Vegetable Pasta Bake

Ingredients:

  • Leftover pasta
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Cheese for topping
  • Tomato or cream sauce

Steps:

  1. Mix pasta, vegetables, and sauce.
  2. Transfer to baking dish and sprinkle cheese on top.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes until golden.
Chicken Rice Soup

Ingredients:

  • Leftover rice
  • Leftover chicken
  • Broth or water
  • Egg (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil broth and add shredded chicken.
  2. Add leftover rice and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. Optional: swirl in beaten egg for extra texture.
Breakfast Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Leftover bread slices
  • Cheese
  • Leftover ham or bacon
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Toast bread slices.
  2. Cook egg as desired and assemble sandwich with cheese and ham.
  3. Serve warm.
Vegetable Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • Leftover vegetables
  • Noodles
  • Broth or water
  • Soy sauce or seasoning

Steps:

  1. Boil broth and add vegetables.
  2. Add noodles and cook until tender.
  3. Season and serve hot.
Potato and Cheese Frittata

Ingredients:

  • Leftover potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Onions or herbs

Steps:

  1. Mix eggs, diced potatoes, cheese, and onions.
  2. Pour mixture into a pan and cook on medium heat.
  3. Flip carefully and cook until firm and golden.
Bean and Rice Patties

Ingredients:

  • Leftover rice
  • Leftover beans
  • Tomato paste or diced tomato
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Mash beans and mix with rice and tomato.
  2. Shape into patties.
  3. Pan-fry until crisp.
Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • Stale bread slices
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Cheese or leftover pesto
  • Fresh herbs

Steps:

  1. Toast bread slices.
  2. Top with tomatoes, cheese, and herbs.
  3. Serve as snack or appetizer.
Leftover Meat Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Leftover meat (chicken, beef, or pork)
  • Vegetables
  • Wraps or tortillas
  • Sauce or dressing

Steps:

  1. Warm the wrap.
  2. Fill with meat, vegetables, and sauce.
  3. Roll tightly and serve.

Leftover Curry Rice Casserole

Ingredients:

  • Leftover rice

Steps:

  • 2 eggs
