Reduce Food Waste Tips

At Home

  • Store food properly to extend freshness.
  • Use leftovers creatively in new meals.
  • Understand date labels ("best by" vs "use by")

Shopping Smart

  • Make a shopping list and stick to it
  • Buy imperfect produce (it tastes the same!)
  • Avoid bulk purchases unless you'll use it all

Storage Tips

  • Keep potatoes and onions in a cool, dark place
  • Store herbs in water like flowers
  • Keep bananas separate from other fruits

Meal Planning

  • Plan meals for the week before shopping
  • Cook the right portions for your household
  • Designate a "use it up" day for leftovers
